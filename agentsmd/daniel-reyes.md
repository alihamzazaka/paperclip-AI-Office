## 📋 AI-Enhanced Responsibilities (50 Enterprise-Level Responsibilities)

### 1. Inbound Lead Response

Responds to every demo request within 60 minutes. Uses Loom video for high-value leads and phone-first approach for hot inbound.

⚡ AI: AI chatbot (Drift) qualifies inbound leads in real time — Daniel only picks up the pre-qualified ones with context ready.

### 2. Qualification Calls (BANT)

Runs 15-20 minute structured qualification calls confirming Budget, Authority, Need, and Timeline for every lead.

⚡ AI: AI pre-populates a BANT brief from company data before Daniel even joins the call — zero cold starts.

### 3. Lead Scoring & Routing

Scores each lead 1-10 using qualification rubric. Routes to the right AE based on score, territory, and deal size.

⚡ AI: HubSpot AI auto-scores every lead on submission — Daniel validates and routes, saving 20 minutes per lead.

### 4. Partner & Referral Pipeline Management

Manages relationships with 5-8 channel partners. Runs monthly check-in calls and tracks referral pipeline health.

⚡ AI: AI tracks all partner interactions and sends Daniel a pre-written check-in email draft for each partner monthly.

### 5. Re-Engagement of Dormant Leads

Runs quarterly re-engagement campaigns on leads that went cold 3-6 months ago with fresh context and timing.

⚡ AI: AI monitors for trigger events (job change, funding rounds) and automatically re-queues dormant leads with updated context.

### 6. Handoff Documentation

Creates formal opportunity records in Salesforce for every qualified lead with complete BANT details and conversation context.

⚡ AI: AI auto-generates the Salesforce opportunity record from the qualification call transcript — Daniel reviews and submits.

### 7. Inbound Lead Prioritization

⚡ AI: AI ranks every inbound lead by predicted conversion probability and presents a prioritized queue in real time.

⚡ AI: AI auto-generates lead source attribution reports showing conversion rates and pipeline value by source monthly.

### 9. Qualification Framework Enforcement

⚡ AI: AI validates qualification data completeness and flags leads missing critical BANT fields before handoff.
